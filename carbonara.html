Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

A 15-min creamy, authentic, and easy-to-make Spaghetti Carbonara.

If you're broke like I am, you will love this 5-ingredient Spaghetti Carbonara to save you in a busy weekday!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • 1 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

The Method:

  1. Boil water in a large pot, heavily salt the pasta water and add olive oil to avoid the past sticking together.
  2. Cook the pancetta starting with a cold pan and allow the fat to render. Turn off heat and set aside when browning is reached.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together 2 eggs, parmesan cheese, and salt & pepper.
  4. When the pasta is cooked to al dente, transfer the pasta onto the pan with the pancetta, mix the pasta with the rendered fat. Turn off the heat.
  5. With the heat turned off, pour the egg-cheese mixture onto the pasta, and continuously stir together until a glossy, creamy sauce thickens over the pasta.
  6. Add pasta water to loosen, add parmesan cheese to thicken the sauce. Experiment till desired consistency of sauce is reached.

Serve on a plate, top with a garnish of your choice, and enjoy your authentic Spaghetti Carbonara!
