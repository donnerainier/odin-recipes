Sinigang Recipe

A classic Filipino dish that will soothe the soul.

A little bit of time to prepare the ingredients will yield a hearty, tangy soup that is sure to have special place in your heart.

Ingredients:

  • Chopped pork back ribs
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced napa cabbage, or spinach, or lettuce
  • 1-2 Packs of Tamarind mix
  • Sliced radishes
  • Optional: garlic
  • Beef broth & water.

The Method:

  1. In a large pot, saute the garlic and onion. Then add the sliced pork back ribs.
  2. Once the ribs are browned, fill the pot with water and beef broth. Let simmer.
  3. Once simmering, add the sliced tomatoes and radishes.
  4. Skim the scum and let boil and simmer for 45 mins-1 hour. Then add tamarind mix.
  5. Add the greens and simmer for another 10 mins.
  6. Serve while hot, typically on rice. Enjoy!

This simple soup will hug and keep you warm during those long, cold nights.
