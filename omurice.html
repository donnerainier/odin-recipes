Classic Omurice Recipe

A fulfilling breakfast or lunch meal to power through your day.

The Broque-style omurice is quick to make, fulfilling, and tasty for your appetite and budget!

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Day-old rice
  • Beef strips
  • Julienned carrots
  • Diced Onions
  • Chopped garlic
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • oil

The Method:

  1. With a wok, or pan, heat 1 tbsp of oil, and add the garlic, onions and carrots.
  2. When the onions are translucent, add the beef.
  3. Add the ketchup and soy sauce to the mix.
  4. Turn the heat to high, and add the rice to stir with the rest. Set aside after 2-3 min.
  5. Whisk the eggs. For a flufflier omelette, whisk milk and chunks of butter with the eggs. Pour to a separate pan with medium heat and cook.
  6. Serve the fried rice on a bowl or plate.
  7. Let the omelette sit atop of the rice. Eat while hot.

Dig in and enjoy your Broque-style omurice!
